# Disclaimer

This MCP server provides livestock management data for informational purposes only.

## Not Professional Advice

The data served by this tool does not constitute professional veterinary, animal welfare, or livestock management advice. Welfare codes, movement rules, stocking densities, and other data points are based on published UK government, DEFRA, APHA, and AHDB sources, but individual farm conditions vary. Always consult a qualified veterinarian or livestock advisor before making animal welfare, health, or management decisions.

## Welfare Codes Are Summary Only

Welfare standard data in this server summarises the DEFRA Codes of Practice. The codes contain additional context, exceptions, and species-specific detail that may not be captured here. Always check the full DEFRA welfare code for the species in question.

## Movement Rules Are Summary Only

Movement standstill periods and reporting requirements summarise APHA regulations. Rules vary by species, holding type, and disease status. TB-restricted holdings have additional restrictions. Always check current APHA guidance before moving animals.

## Currency

Agricultural regulations and welfare codes change. This tool shows the data available at the time of its last ingestion run. Check `check_data_freshness` for the age of the data.
